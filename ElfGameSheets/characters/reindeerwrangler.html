Character Sheet

Role: Reindeer Wrangler

Your work with Santa's Reindeer has given you bond

Starting Elves: 10

Abilities:

Level 1:

Carrot Bribery (Active):
You can spend 50 wood to hire 3 of Santa's Reindeer to assist you for the day (they count as elves), this cost goes up by 5 for each use. The Reindeer are immune bad weather.

Level 2:

Carrot Bribery (Active):
You can spend 70 wood to hire 6 of Santa's Reindeer to assist you for the day (they count as elves), this cost goes up by 5 for each use. The Reindeer are immune to weather however will not go to The Beyond the Trench Taiga
Reindeer Rescue (Active):
You may instead buy the help of the reindeer to save the same number of elves (same cost rules apply)

Level 3:

Delicious Carrot Bribery (Active):
You may now hire all 9 of Santa's Reindeer for 105 wood (still going up by 5 each use) and they will travel as far as The Beyond the Trench Taiga
Reindeer Rescue (Active):
You may instead buy the help of the reindeer to save the same number of elves (same cost rules apply)
