Character Sheet

Role: Mystic

You channel the ghost of Christmas past, present and future through your magical cards

Starting Elves: 10

Abilities:

Level 1/2/3:

Magical Christmas Deck (Passive/ Active):
When you level up draw 2 cards from your Magical Christmas Deck. You may chose one card to keep and you may play it at any time. However if any of them are Cracker Cards those abilities active immediately and you keep any remaining cards.
(Hint: You can use cards in combination so it may be worthwhile holding onto your cards for the right moment)
