Character Sheet

Role: Winter Witch

Is it really so cold to use your magic or is everyone else a little hot headed

Starting Elves: 13

Abilities:

Level 1:

Winter Magic (Passive):
Whenever bad weather occurs gain 2 points of Winter Magic

Level 2:

Winter Magic (Passive):
Whenever bad weather occurs gain 2 points of Winter Magic
Raise the dead (Active):
You may expend a point of your Winter Magic to bring back your dead elves as zombie elves although they will not go back to The Beyond the Trench Taiga

Level 3:

Winter Magic (Passive):
Whenever bad weather occurs gain 2 points of Winter Magic
Raise the dead (Active):
You may expend a point of your Winter Magic to bring back your dead elves as zombie elves although they will not go back to The Beyond the Trench Taiga
Icy Grasp (Active):
You may use your Winter Magic to charm someone else's elves. For each point of Winter Magic expended you may "borrow" an elf from any player for 1 day
