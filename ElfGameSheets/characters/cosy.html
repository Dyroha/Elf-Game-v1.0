Character Sheet

Role: Cosy Elf

Snuggle up, get warm, have a cup of hot chocolate and relax

Starting Elves: 5

Abilities:

Level 1:

Close to Home (Passive)
Roll d8s instead of d6s when gathering wood from the nearby forest

Level 2:

Close to Home (Passive):
Roll d8s instead of d6s when gathering wood from the nearby forest
Warm Campfire (Single Use Active):
Must be used before the weather roll and before any other abilities are used. Cause all elves to work in the nearby wood for 1 day.

Level 3:

Very Close to Home (Passive):
Roll 3 d8s and take the best 2 when gathering wood from the nearby forest
Warm Campfire (Single Use Active):
Must be used before the weather roll and before any other abilities are used. Cause all elves to work in the nearby wood for 1 day.
A good rest (Passive):
Instead of the elves striking you may allow them a rest day and get double the haul the next day
