Character Sheet

Role: Meteorologist

You are developing you skills of weather prediction to support Santa

Starting Elves: 10

Skill Rank: 7

Abilities:

Level 1:

Learning on the Job (Passive):
Gain a Prediction Point for every good weather that occurs
Weather Prediction (Active):
Expend 2 Prediction Points to give advantage on the next weather roll. Decrease Skill Rank by 1 for each use

Level 2:

Learning on the Job (Passive):
Gain a Prediction Point for every good weather that occurs
Weather Prediction (Active):
Expend 2 Prediction Points to give advantage on the next weather roll. Decrease Skill Rank by 1 for each use

Level 3:

Learning on the Job (Passive):
Gain a Prediction Point for every good weather that occurs
Weather Prediction (Active):
Expend 2 Prediction Points to give advantage on the next weather roll. Decrease Skill Rank by 1 for each use
Weather Control (Active):
Expend Prediction Points equal to your Skill Rank to give yourself good weather for today (can be used after the weather roll)
